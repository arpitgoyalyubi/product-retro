Feature 2025-11-19
UPI Reverse Penny Drop — Bank Verification

Ops review queue fell from 15.1% → 4.3% — a 3.5× reduction in manual work per bank verification. RPD is now 77% of all bank verifications (573/week). Two-part ship: optional Nov 19; default path from KYC redesign Dec 22.
Funnel drop-off was not the lever: PAN→Bank completion held flat (~87.9% before → ~83.2% after — same fraction of users clear the step in both eras). RPD's win is verification quality & ops load, not step conversion.
